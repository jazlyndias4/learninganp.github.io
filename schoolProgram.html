School Program

Our school program will be held at several elementary schools in the Bay Area. We will either be hosting it during the school’s lunches or after school. Like the library program, each program will be 4 consecutive weeks long and each week will consist of a lecture, activity or craft, and worksheets about various anatomical systems!
